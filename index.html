How It Works

Multi-Modal Detection.
Zero Cloud Dependency.

Each Onyx Grid node fuses Acoustic Intelligence with Edge AI Vision, identifying drone signatures that traditional systems miss entirely. By processing data locally on the device, the system delivers instant detection without requiring constant high-bandwidth connectivity.

Acoustic signatures are cross-referenced with AI-driven visual analysis at the edge, producing robust detections even in adverse conditions — in the dark, through foliage, or under electronic interference.

The Threat

The Airspace Security Gap Is Real.

The rapid proliferation of low-cost, low-altitude drones has created a significant vulnerability for critical infrastructure, private property, and tactical zones. Existing solutions are either too expensive, too visible, or too dependent on infrastructure that can be jammed.

🏭
Critical infrastructure exposed to surveillance and payload delivery threats.
📡
Traditional radar systems emit detectable signals and are prohibitively costly to deploy at scale.
🔇
Low-cost drones exploit gaps in cellular and Wi-Fi reliant detection systems.
🛡️
Onyx Grid provides a persistent, 24/7 "digital canopy" — early warning before threats become crises.

Invisible & Resilient

Unlike active radars, Onyx Grid is a fully passive system. It emits zero detectable signals. Operating over a redundant LoRa Mesh Network, the system remains online even when cellular networks and Wi-Fi are jammed or unavailable — the scenarios where detection matters most.

02 / 03
⚡
Cost-Efficient Scaling

Our decentralized architecture enables rapid deployment of dozens of sensor nodes at a fraction of the cost of a single military-grade radar station. Complex terrains, vast perimeters, and urban environments — all covered with no blind spots and no massive infrastructure investment.

03 / 03
🔒
Edge AI Privacy & Speed

By analyzing audio and video locally on each node, Onyx Grid guarantees maximum data privacy — no raw footage leaves the device. With zero round-trip cloud latency, alerts are dispatched in seconds, not minutes, giving security teams the time advantage they need.
