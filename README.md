# onyxgrid-web
Autonomous Edge Intelligence for Airspace Security
Onyx Grid is a decentralized infrastructure of smart sensor nodes designed to detect, track, and alert against unauthorized UAV (drone) activity in real-time.

1. What it does
Onyx Grid utilizes a multi-modal approach to drone detection. Each node combines Acoustic Intelligence with Edge AI Vision to identify drone signatures that traditional systems miss. By processing data locally on the device (Edge Computing), it ensures instant detection without the need for constant high-bandwidth cloud connectivity.

2. Why it is needed
The rapid proliferation of low-cost, low-altitude drones has created a significant security gap for critical infrastructure, private property, and tactical zones. Onyx Grid provides a persistent, 24/7 "digital canopy" that monitors the airspace, providing early warning alerts to security teams before a threat becomes a crisis.

3. Key Advantages
Invisible & Resilient: Unlike active radars, Onyx Grid is a passive system. It emits no detectable signals and operates via a redundant Mesh Network (LoRa), meaning it stays online even if cellular networks or Wi-Fi are jammed or unavailable.

Cost-Efficient Scaling: Our decentralized architecture allows for the rapid deployment of dozens of nodes at a fraction of the cost of a single military-grade radar station, covering vast or complex terrains with no blind spots.

Edge AI Privacy & Speed: By analyzing sound and video locally on the node, we ensure maximum data privacy and eliminate latency. Alerts are sent in seconds, not minutes.
